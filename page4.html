Featured Artist

Audrey Kathleen Ruston (Audrey Hepburn)


Born on May 4, 1929 in Belgium, rose a future star, Audrey Hepburn, the daughter of Baroness Ella Van Heemstra and Joseph Victor Anthony Ruston.

Audrey soon became an inspiration to many as she was a representation of many women when they were never given an opportunity to take the action of leading for the people.

Her career as a model, actress, a spokesperson, and an advocate of youth solidified her title as a woman of inspiration.

As a child she came from a divorced parents. As a result, she had to move to London along with her mother and start of a new life in a private school.

Eventually, when Audrey and her mother went to the Netherlands, she left herself in a depressed state because of the Nazi occupation.

Given this event, she used it as her motivation to start her career as soon as she went back to London. Numerous projects from acting, modeling, etc. was on her way.
